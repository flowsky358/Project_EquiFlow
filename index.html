Health & Dignity

No girl should miss school because of her period.
We donate pads, build safe facilities, and run health education programs that break stigma and keep girls in class.

Leadership & Community

Girls connect, lead, and lift each other up.
Public speaking workshops, peer mentorship networks, and school supplies that give girls the tools and confidence to thrive.

Advocacy
