What is a Chapter?

An EquiFlow chapter is a local hub of our movement. Whether in a middle school, high school, or college, chapters are the boots-on-the-ground force that organizes drives and builds awareness.

Local Community

Form a team of 5-50+ passionate students.

Direct Impact

Lead fundraising drives and advocacy workshops.
