What Is a Chapter?

The boots on
the ground for
real change

An EquiFlow chapter is a student-run group at your school or community center. You become the local face of the movement — organizing supply drives, spreading awareness, and connecting your community to girls who need support.

Build Your Team Gather a founding board of passionate students ready to lead change in your school.
Take Direct Action Lead supply drives, fundraisers, and awareness campaigns that send real resources to real girls.
Join a Global Network Connect with other chapter leaders worldwide and be part of something much bigger than your school.

How It Works

Four steps to
launch

Your journey to becoming a Chapter President starts here. It's simpler than you think.

Step 01

Build Your Core

Gather at least 5 friends to serve as your founding executive board. You need a president, secretary, and a few passionate members.

Step 02

Get Approved

Register as an official club at your school or community center. Most schools have a simple form — your advisor can help.

Step 03

Register With Us

Complete our onboarding form and you'll receive your official Chapter Starter Kit — templates, guides, and everything you need.

Step 04

Launch Day

Host your first meeting, plan your first drive, and officially become part of the EquiFlow global network.
