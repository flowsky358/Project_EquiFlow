Who We Are

We go where the
need is greatest.

Project EquiFlow is a global nonprofit working in the world's most underserved communities — providing not just access to education, but the tools, mentorship, and community every girl needs to rise.

Inspired by movements supporting Afghan women's right to learn, we take that urgency worldwide. Because every girl — regardless of geography — deserves the chance to lead her own future.

Why We Exist

Mission, Vision & Goal

Three commitments that guide every program, partnership, and decision we make.

Goal

Equal education for every girl —
no exceptions.

Dismantling the barriers of poverty, geography, and gender that keep girls out of classrooms worldwide. We exist to make this a reality — one community at a time.

Mission

Educate. Lead. Connect.

Empowering girls and women in underserved communities by expanding access to education and creating real opportunities for growth, leadership, and connection — so every girl can define her own future.

Vision

A world where every girl is valued, respected, and leading.

We envision a world where women and girls have equitable access to school, work, and leadership — everywhere. Where no girl's potential is limited by where she was born.

The Ripple Effect

Every girl we educate, every barrier we break, and every stigma we dismantle creates ripples that extend far beyond her classroom.

When we put a girl in school, we change how entire communities think about women's potential. When we develop young leaders, we shift generational expectations about what girls can accomplish. When we break stigmas together, we build a foundation of dignity that lasts long after our work is done.
