Whether you love art or love people — there's a place for you at Project EquiFlow. Our volunteers create real, tangible things that land in the hands of girls around the world.

Step 1

Choose your
team

We have two volunteer tracks, each making a direct difference for girls in our programs. Pick the one that fits you — or apply for both.

Creative Track

Arts Team

Our Arts Team creates handmade and digital motivational cards that get sent directly to girls in our programs — reminding them they are seen, valued, and capable of anything.

  • Design motivational cards for girls in our programs
  • Work in any medium — illustration, painting, digital art
  • Collaborate with teammates on themed card collections
  • Your art ships to real girls around the world
Outreach Track

Outreach Team

Our Outreach Team is the connective tissue of EquiFlow — spreading our mission, building community relationships, and helping make sure no girl gets left behind.

  • Raise awareness for our programs in your community
  • Coordinate local supply and product donation drives
  • Represent EquiFlow at school and community events
  • Help grow our network of supporters and partners
Step 2

Get your first
assignment

Once your application is reviewed, you'll be welcomed into our team hub where your team lead will walk you through your first project and connect you with fellow volunteers.

Assignments are matched to your availability and skill level — so whether you have two hours a week or twenty, there's always something meaningful waiting for you.

Your Assignment Inbox

After you're onboarded, you'll have access to open projects from both teams. Claim what excites you, submit your work, and watch it turn into something real.

  • Arts: monthly card collection themes posted by your team lead
  • Outreach: upcoming drives, events, and campaigns to join
  • Real-time communication with your team and leads
  • Recognition for standout work each month
Step 3

Log your service
hours

Every assignment you complete counts. We track your hours and provide official documentation so your volunteer work is recognized — at school, on applications, and beyond.

Official Recognition

Volunteer assignments are eligible for service hours and can be submitted for formal recognition, including:

  • Presidential Volunteer Service Award (PVSA) tracking
  • High school and university volunteer credit
  • NHS and community service program hours
  • Official EquiFlow verification letter on request
