Ways to Volunteer

Find your role

Three ways to get involved — pick the one that fits where you are and how much time you have.

Core Member

Core Member

Our most hands-on volunteers. Core Members work directly on programs, content, and operations to keep EquiFlow running.

  • Support program planning and execution
  • Create content, campaigns, and materials
  • Collaborate weekly with the EquiFlow team
  • Earn official volunteer service hours
Apply to Join
Ambassador

Ambassador

Ambassadors spread the EquiFlow mission in their schools and communities, growing our reach and impact from the ground up.

  • Represent EquiFlow at events and in school
  • Run awareness campaigns and donation drives
  • Share our mission on social media
  • Connect us with local organizations and partners

Chapter Leader

Start a Chapter

Lead a local EquiFlow chapter at your school or in your city. You'll build a team, run events, and bring our mission to your community.

  • Launch an official EquiFlow chapter
  • Lead a team of local volunteers
  • Host fundraisers and awareness events
  • Get full support and materials from us

Get Involved

Host an event
for girls.

If you are interested in hosting an EquiFlow fundraiser, a free health and dignity workshop, a conference, or a speaking engagement in your school or community, we would love to make it happen.

We can help you plan it, provide materials, and connect you with our team. All you need is the passion to show up.

Email us to get started

equiflow.connect@gmail.com

Fundraiser

Host a drive or fundraiser at your school, place of worship, or workplace. Every dollar goes directly to girls in our programs.

Health & Dignity Workshop

Organize a free workshop in your community focused on girls' health, hygiene, and dignity. We'll provide resources and support.

Conference or Speaking Engagement

Invite an EquiFlow speaker to your school, conference, or event to share our mission and inspire others to act.

Submit a Written Piece

Have something to say about girls' education or health? Email us a PDF of your article. If accepted, it will be featured on our blog and partner platforms.

Start a Chapter

Bring EquiFlow to
your community

Starting a chapter is how you turn your school or city into a hub for girls' empowerment. Here is how it works.

1

Reach Out

Email us at equiflow.connect@gmail.com with your name, school or city, and why you want to start a chapter.

2

Get Onboarded

We will schedule a quick call to walk you through our chapter model, share materials, and answer all your questions.

3

Build Your Team

Recruit a small group of passionate people in your school or city who want to volunteer alongside you and grow the chapter.

4

Launch & Lead

Host your first event, run campaigns, and represent EquiFlow officially. We will support you every step of the way.
