Get Involved

Three ways to change a girl's future.

Give, volunteer, or partner with us — every action you take keeps a girl in school and in leadership.

Way One  ·  Volunteer

Join the team building this movement.

We are students, advocates, and organizers who show up in person and online. Whether you want to join our core team, become an ambassador, or start a chapter at your school — there is a role for you.

Join the Core Team → Become an Ambassador → Start a Chapter →
Way Two  ·  Partner

Host an event. Amplify our reach.

Partner with us to bring EquiFlow's mission to your community. We work with schools, organizations, and individuals to host fundraisers, health workshops, and speaking engagements that expand our impact.

Host a Fundraiser → Host a Workshop → Book a Speaker →
Way Three  ·  Give

Your dollars go directly to girls.

Every dollar you donate removes a financial barrier standing between a girl and her education. We fund school supplies, scholarships, and menstrual health products — the things that keep girls in school.
