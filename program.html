Our Programs

Education is a right.
We make it a reality.

From local classrooms to global communities — we remove every barrier between a girl and her future.

Girls with 12 years of education earn up to 10× more and are far less likely to marry young or experience poverty.
World Bank & UNESCO, 2023
Our Approach

Three Routes to Education Equality

1

Pillar One

Health & Dignity

  • Menstrual product donations — pads, reusable kits, and hygiene supplies
  • Health education workshops on menstrual health and well-being
  • School partnerships to install free period product dispensers
  • Community conversations that break stigma and normalize menstruation
2

Pillar Two

Leadership & Community

  • Leadership workshops on public speaking, problem-solving, and teamwork
  • Mentorship connecting girls with peers and role models
  • Community projects where girls identify and solve local challenges
  • School supplies and resources to help girls come prepared
  • A supportive network where girls build confidence together
3

Pillar Three

Advocacy

  • Partnering with schools to expand access to menstrual health resources
  • Community campaigns that reduce stigma around girls' education
  • Working with local leaders to improve educational opportunities
  • Sharing program data to advocate for evidence-based solutions
  • Empowering girls to use their voices to create change
Our Reach

We show up locally and think globally.

Local Impact

In Our Community

We show up where it matters most — in our own schools and neighborhoods. Through in-person events and direct donations, we make an immediate difference close to home.

  • Leadership & empowerment workshops
  • Donations to underfunded school districts
  • Menstrual health product drives
  • Community awareness campaigns
National & International

Beyond Our Borders

Girls' education is a global challenge. We partner with organizations and communities across the country and around the world to amplify our impact at scale.

  • Scholarships & school supplies abroad
  • Partnerships with global education nonprofits
  • Advocacy for girls' education policy
  • Fundraising for international programs

Pillar One  ·  Education Access

Every girl deserves a seat in the classroom.

We remove every financial barrier standing between a girl and her education.

  • School Supplies Books, uniforms, and everything a girl needs to show up ready.
  • Scholarships Direct funding so money is never a reason to stop learning.
  • Long-Term Support We follow each girl from primary school through higher education.

Pillar Two  ·  Leadership & Community

Education is just the beginning of her power.

We teach girls to lead, speak up, and build community.

  • Leadership Workshops Public speaking, problem-solving, and civic engagement.
  • Mentorship Networks Girls connect with mentors who have walked the same path.
  • Community Roles Graduates step into real leadership positions immediately.

Pillar Three  ·  Health & Dignity

A period should never end a girl's education.

1 in 5 girls misses school every month because of her period. We fix that.

  • Product Donations Pads, reusable kits, and hygiene supplies delivered directly.
  • Open Conversations We break stigma by talking openly about menstrual health in schools.
  • Safe Facilities Private, dignified spaces so girls can manage their health at school.
